about us

our mission

ADJUSTING TO COLLEGE CAN BE DIFFICULT. TAKING CARE OF YOURSELF FOR THE FIRST TIME CAN BE DIFFICULT. LEARNING HOW TO COOK CAN BE DIFFICULT.

Here, we hope to assist those who just moved away from home learn how to cook and manage their meals by themselves for the first time.

We want to create a fun and stimulating environment for those to learn, while minimizing food waste associated with cooking. With this sustainable and fun model to learn how to cook, it enables students to take control of their eating habits

Game

How it works

Join a cooking simulator, where you attempt to create a meal by yourself. You're welcome to use the sample recipes provided as a starting point. Go around the kitchen, gathering your ingredients from the correct locations. Next, process the food the way you want, including but not limited to boiling, chopping, and mixing. Finally, present the dish you have made and get a rating.
